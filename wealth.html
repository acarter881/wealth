The American Dream

Work hard and become wealthy?

Maybe so, but work alone might not cut it. The following graphs [1] show
various paths to prosperity.

Education

More advanced schooling helps. If you can avoid the crushing debt of student loans, all the better. Try to find a major that
you're good at and that is good to you.

Birthright

For accruing wealth, what's even better than education? Being born wealthy. In the graph below, note how the birthright high
enders are able to become more wealthy than the education high enders. So before being born, shop around.
